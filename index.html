MF SIP Tracker
Link a SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) to any mutual fund holding. Click the SIP button in the fund's row to set the monthly amount and SIP date.

Once set, the SIP badge appears in the fund row and the summary bar shows your total monthly SIP commitment. SIP dates also appear on the Financial Calendar.
How to add & track Mutual Funds
Step-by-step:
  1. Type the fund name or AMC in the search box (e.g., "Axis Bluechip" or "Parag Parikh").
  2. All 16,000+ AMFI-registered schemes load automatically — pick yours from the dropdown.
  3. Enter your total units held and average purchase NAV (check your CAS statement or broker app).
  4. Click Add Holding.
  5. Click ↻ Refresh NAV to fetch today's NAV and see your current value and P&L.
Tip: Refresh NAV once a day after market close (after 6 PM IST) for the most up-to-date NAV.

Emergency Fund Tracker
What is an Emergency Fund?
An emergency fund is a dedicated savings reserve that covers your living expenses in case of unexpected events — job loss, medical emergency, major repair, etc.

How it works here:
  1. Enter your current emergency fund balance.
  2. Set your target — typically 6 months of expenses (the financial planning standard).
  3. The app auto-calculates your average monthly expenses from the last 3 months in the Track tab.
  4. The gauge shows how many months of coverage you currently have.
Green = target reached  ·  Amber = on track  ·  Red = needs attention.

Debt Payoff Planner
How Debt Payoff Works
This planner uses your loans (added in Portfolio → Loans tab) to suggest the most efficient repayment order.

Snowball Method: Pay off the smallest outstanding balance first. Once cleared, roll that payment into the next. Provides quick wins and psychological motivation.

Avalanche Method: Pay off the highest interest rate loan first. Mathematically saves the most total interest over time.

Both methods assume you pay minimum EMIs on all loans and put extra money toward the priority loan. The Avalanche method usually saves more money; Snowball can be more motivating.
